About Us

Decades of Excellence.

Founded in December 1980 by Walter J. Vestal, Vestal Steel Specialties, Inc. is a Texas Corporation that does structural and custom steel fabrication; custom shearing; and forming. We work with hot and cold rolled structural members, plate, sheet, aluminum, stainless and galvanized metal.

Building Relationships

Vestal Steel serves over 250 customers, large and small. Our experience and expertise spans the spectrum of projects for contractors, municipalities, utilities, individuals, and everything in between. We have fabricated entire buildings, handrails, guardrails, monument signs, etc. as well as countless custom projects.

Across America

While the majority of our work is within a one-hundred-mile radius, we have shipped jobs all over Texas, the United States and overseas.
