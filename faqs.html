FAQ's

Do you work with stainless and aluminum?

Yes. We have fabricated numerous stainless & aluminum handrails, guardrails, etc. as well as custom fabrications.

Do you work for contractors only?

No. We have a large walk-in customer base as well as many jobshop type repeat customers.

What is your handling capacity?

We have two crane bays with two overhead cranes in each bay up to 10 ton capacity each. Also 4 each 6,000# forklifts and one 18,000# forklift.
